Child Safety & Protection Standards

AlpConnect strictly prohibits any content involving sexual abuse, exploitation, or endangerment of minors.

Prohibited Content

  • Sexual content involving minors
  • Sexual violence against children
  • Attempts to contact minors for sexual purposes

Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app. Additionally, reports can be sent via email:

Email: deiss.thomas@gmx.de

Enforcement

Reported content is reviewed and removed immediately if it violates our policies. Accounts involved may be suspended or permanently banned. Severe cases will be reported to relevant authorities.
